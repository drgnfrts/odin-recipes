Mee Hoon Kueh

Description

Classic Malaysian/Singaporean hand-torn noodles in a pork broth, paired with ground pork, spinach, egg and ikan bilis. Good for those cold rainy days! As iisted from Miss Tam Chiak

Ingredients

  • For the dough
    • 300 g All Purpose Flour
    • 120 ml Water
    • 1 Egg
    • A pinch of salt
    • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • For the dish
    • 200 g Minced Pork>
    • 4 Dried Mushrooms
    • A bunch of Spinach
    • 1.5 l Pork Broth
    • 3 cloves Garlic

Steps

  1. Add in egg, salt and oil into the flour.
  2. Knead the flour mixture into a dough, adding water gradually until it forms a soft dough.
  3. Shape into a ball, cover with cling wrap to rest for 1 hour.
  4. Pinch the dough into small pieces and blanch the mee hoon kueh in boiling water. Set aside.
  5. Air fry the Ikan Bilis at 200-degree Celsius for 5 minutes.
  6. Marinate the minced meat with salt, pepper, sesame oil and cornstarch.
  7. Heat in minced garlic and mushrooms. Fry till fragrant.
  8. Add in pork broth and minced pork. Bring to boil.
  9. Add in blanched mee hoon kueh and spinach. Once the vegetables are cooked, crack an egg into the soup.
  10. Top noodles with fried Ikan Bilis and serve with red chillies.
